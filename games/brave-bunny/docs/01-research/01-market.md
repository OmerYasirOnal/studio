# Market overview — action-roguelite (mobile)

> Synthesis of public industry signals as of 2026-05-12. All claims are third-party estimates unless otherwise noted; see `04-references.md` for the citation list.

## Genre at a glance

The "auto-battler action roguelite" / "bullet heaven" category was effectively created on mobile by **Habby's Survivor.io** (2022) which ported and monetized **poncle's Vampire Survivors** (2021) formula. The wave continued with **Archero** (2019, Habby's earlier hit), various clones (e.g. **Brotato**, **Halls of Torment**, **Soulstone Survivors**) and Habby's **Magic Survival**, **Capybara Go!**, **Frost Rogue**.

Defining traits:
- Single-thumb / touch-only controls (joystick or auto-walk)
- 7-15 minute run length
- Random level-up draft of upgrades (typically 3-of-N)
- Weapon evolution / synergy crafting as build depth
- Persistent meta-progression between runs
- Pacing built around escalating swarm density + boss waves
- Mobile-IAP + rewarded-ads + battle pass monetization

## Revenue & install signal

- **Survivor.io** has been the category's top earner since launch, with multiple third-party estimates placing global revenue in the high-hundreds of millions to >$1B lifetime as of 2025-2026. Daily download counts in millions during early ad-spend peaks.
- **Archero** crossed the $1B lifetime revenue mark before 2024 per Sensor Tower (paywall) and AppMagic public dashboards.
- **Vampire Survivors** sold ~5M units on Steam alone by 2023 and is on Apple Arcade subscription as well as paid mobile (~$1.99 / $4.99 region-dependent).
- The category is **mid-LTV but high-D1**: D1 retention in the 35-45% band is the table-stakes target for soft-launch greenlight on mobile.

## Audience

- **Geography:** Strongest in Asia (KR / TW / SEA) and emerging markets (TR / PH / ID / BR / MX) for download volume; US / EU dominate ARPU. China (iOS only) is a separate market because Android is fragmented and Google Play is absent.
- **Demographics:** Skews male 18-35, but Habby titles (Survivor.io especially) show broader 25-45 reach. Casual-with-depth players who lapsed from Clash Royale / Coin Master.
- **Device:** iPhone 12-class baseline is industry-standard for 60 fps targeting; iPhone SE 3 covers the small-screen safe-area edge.

## Soft-launch markets — TR / PH / ID

| Market | Why interesting | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **Turkey (TR)** | High install per CPM, vocal community, Habby has historically tested here. Native language matters for D7. | Currency volatility distorts ARPU comparisons. |
| **Philippines (PH)** | English-fluent, mobile-first, Habby's stable test market. Strong word-of-mouth via TikTok. | Low ARPU; ads matter more than IAP at launch. |
| **Indonesia (ID)** | Largest SEA mobile market by DAU. Big appetite for cartoon/cute aesthetic. | iOS share is small; Android ad fill is the lever. |

These three are the canonical Habby-style soft-launch trio: low CAC, predictive of broader SEA performance, English-bypass tolerable for early stats.

## Ad networks dominant in genre

- **Unity Ads** + **AdMob** are baseline.
- **Mintegral**, **AppLovin** lead for SEA / TR Android.
- **ironSource** common for IAP-heavy KR / JP.

## Why brave-bunny exists

Looking at the three deconstructions in `02-competitors/`:

- **Survivor.io** dominates on systems polish but its grim-zombie palette is the genre's weakest moat in family-friendly markets.
- **Vampire Survivors** owns the loss-banks-gold dignity loop and the draft-cadence design — but its single-purchase economy doesn't translate to mobile-LTV models.
- **Archero** has the genre's best rewarded-ads ladder but is widely perceived as energy-gated pay-to-win, hurting word-of-mouth.

There is room — explicitly — for a **cartoon-mascot-driven, no-hard-gate, animal-roster action-roguelite** that translates the Habby live-ops machine to a TikTok-shareable visual language. That's the brave-bunny thesis. Positioning details in `03-positioning.md`.

3. Whether **TikTok creator partnerships** are still cost-effective at our scale (one-developer indie) in TR/PH/ID.
