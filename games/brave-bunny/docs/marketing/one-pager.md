---

## Tagline

**Hop. Sword. Survive. — Ten minutes of low-poly chaos that respects your phone, your time, and your wallet.**

---

## Elevator pitch

In **Brave Bunny**, you are a fearless rabbit knight with a tiny sword and big problems. Hordes of foxes, otters and bee-bosses pour into a sun-soaked meadow and you have ten minutes to dance through them. Pick a weapon, watch your numbers climb, evolve your build, and try again. Saturated low-poly cartoon visuals run buttery-smooth on a five-year-old phone. No pay-to-win. No energy gates. No gear gacha. Just one more run.

---

## Key features

- **10-minute runs that respect your day.** Pick up, play one wave, put down. No mandatory grind.
- **Hand-tuned weapon evolutions.** Every weapon has a charm-fused upgrade form, balanced on a TTK ladder rather than vibes.
- **Boss-fight punctuation.** Every two minutes a chunky boss arrives with telegraphed patterns — no DPS check, just dance and dodge.
- **Saturated low-poly art.** A meadow you want to live in: glossy carrots, sun-baked grass, beehive cathedrals. Toon shader, 80-draw-call budget, locked 60 fps on iPhone 12.
- **No pay-to-win, ever.** All purchases are cosmetic, battle pass, or convenience. Public balance pledge in our positioning doc.
- **Offline-first, privacy-first.** Plays without internet. No analytics SDK. No advertising SDK. Telemetry is local and opt-in to share.

## Audience

- **Primary:** mobile action-roguelite players who loved **Survivor.io**, **Vampire Survivors Mobile**, **Brotato**, **Soul Knight** — but bounced off paywalls or grind-walls.
- **Secondary:** lapsed mobile gamers looking for "one short, satisfying loop" rather than 90-minute MMO sessions.
- **Soft-launch geography:** Turkey, Philippines, Indonesia. Chosen for engaged mobile-roguelite communities and lower CPI for performance-pure testing.

## Inspirations (and where we differ)

| Reference | What we borrow | What we change |
|---|---|---|
| Survivor.io | The 10-minute run shape, weapon-evolve mechanic | No power-creep cash shop. No energy. |
| Vampire Survivors | Generous unlock cadence, dense particle joy | Mobile-first input, smaller per-screen entity counts |
| Brotato | Tight loadout choices, run-end celebration | Real-time arena rather than turn-stop |
| Capybara Go | Warm cartoon-mammal cast | Active dodge, not auto-play |
| Soul Knight | Pixel-perfect mobile feel | 3D toon-shader meadow instead of pixel dungeon |

## Business model

- Premium cosmetic shop (skins, hats, emotes, weapon visual reskins). No power.
- Battle pass: ~50 tiers, ≤ $9.99, optional unlock.
- Subscription "Monthly Card" ≤ $4.99/mo, time-saving rewards only.
- **No SKU above $19.99.** **No tier-skip pack above $4.99.** **Decline is always the larger, pre-focused button** on opt-in surfaces.
