# Biome Flavor — Brave Bunny

> Mood paragraphs and on-entry text for each of the 5 biomes. Owner: narrative-designer. Consumers: ui-engineer (intro cards), level-designer (signposts and props), localizer (TR seeds), art-director (atmospheric reference).
>
> Strict tone-bible adherence (`00-tone-bible.md`). Atmosphere through specific concrete detail — no "ancient," "cursed," "dark," "evil," "dead." Hook lines are the 1-line biome-intro UI card shown on entry. Environmental storytelling notes are placement hints for level-designer; no cutscenes.

## Meadow

**Hook line:** "Sunny patch. Bunny pops out, ears up, ready to hop."

**Ambient detail:** The grass is the kind of green that smells warm. A breeze moves the daisies one row at a time, and somewhere off to the left a bee is being very busy about a single flower. The fence posts lean like they have been napping. The carrots are not where they should be, and the trail of little nibble-marks suggests someone has been at them.

**Environmental storytelling notes:**
- A tipped-over wheelbarrow with one carrot still inside, near the start lane.
- A wooden sign in friendly handwriting: "Carrots this way →" — the arrow points to an empty patch.
- A trail of small paw-prints leading off toward the Beach unlock signpost.

**Localization seed (TR):** "Güneşli bir köşe. Tavşan dışarı zıpladı, kulaklar dik, hazır."

## Beach

**Hook line:** "Warm sand, salt in the air, and a crab who looks very busy."

**Ambient detail:** The sand is just hot enough under your paws to be a conversation. Tiny waves rinse and rinse the same line of shells. Crabs scamper sideways like they have urgent errands somewhere to the right. Somewhere a gull is laughing at its own joke. The carrots have washed up in clumps and a few have rolled into the tide pools.

**Environmental storytelling notes:**
- A thatched beach hut with a hand-painted "Open" sign that has clearly been flipped wrong-side-up.
- Three coconuts in a tidy pile, with one carrot wedged hopefully on top.
- A little driftwood arrow stuck in the sand, pointing toward the forest path. Someone scratched "carrots also that way" beside it.

**Localization seed (TR):** "Sıcak kum, tuzlu hava ve epey meşgul görünen bir yengeç."

## Forest

**Hook line:** "Cool shade, leaves whispering. Mind your step under the canopy."

**Ambient detail:** The light falls in coins through the leaves and lands on the moss in pretty patterns. Branches creak in slow conversation overhead. A mushroom near the path has tiny letters carved into its cap, the way kids carve into picnic tables. The carrots have rolled into the ferns, and you can hear at least three small somethings rustling about in there.

**Environmental storytelling notes:**
- Carved hint markers on big mushrooms: "snare patch ahead — go around," in the same friendly handwriting as the Meadow sign.
- A log bridge with one plank loose, a small note tacked to the rail: "Sorry about the plank. — B."
- A toy slingshot, abandoned beside an acorn pile, exactly as a squirrel would leave it.

**Localization seed (TR):** "Serin gölge, yapraklar fısıldıyor. Ağaçların altında adımına dikkat et."

---

## Cavern

**Hook line:** "Glow-mushrooms light the way. Someone's been down here already."

**Ambient detail:** The air goes a little cooler the moment you step in. The glow-mushrooms make soft cyan puddles on the floor and the walls have gems poking out in cheerful clusters. Your paws sound louder than you remember. Somewhere ahead a small drip is keeping perfect time, like a kitchen tap that needs a wrench. The tunnels echo your hops back to you, just half a beat late.

**Environmental storytelling notes:**
- A carrot basket sitting upright on a stone shelf, two carrots still inside — as if someone set it down for a minute and forgot to come back.
- A small lantern hung from a hook in the rock wall, still warm to the touch.
- A trail of glow-mushroom petals scattered across the floor, leading deeper in — someone made a breadcrumb path.

**Localization seed (TR):** "Işıltılı mantarlar yolu aydınlatıyor. Birileri buraya önceden inmiş."

---

## Snow

**Hook line:** "Soft drifts, quiet hills. Footprints lead off toward the pines."

**Ambient detail:** The world goes white and the sound goes away with it. The drifts make round little hills like someone tucked them in for the night. A pine tree shakes off a clump of snow in a soft pat. Far off, a pair of paw-prints stitches a neat line up the hillside and over the next rise. The carrots are wrapped in snow like wrapped presents you have not opened yet.

**Environmental storytelling notes:**
- A small igloo with a kettle steaming beside it; nobody home, but the door flap is freshly brushed.
- A scarf draped on a pine branch, the same red as Bunny's ribbon — someone passed through, in a hurry but careful.
- Foot-tracks leading uphill, joined at one point by a much bigger set of tracks. The bigger set is shaped like a yeti pretending it is not.

**Localization seed (TR):** "Yumuşak kar yığınları, sessiz tepeler. Ayak izleri çamların arkasına doğru gidiyor."
