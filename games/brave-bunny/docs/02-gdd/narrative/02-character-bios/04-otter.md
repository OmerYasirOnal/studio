# Otter

## Silhouette
A sleek brown ribbon with whiskers, a long flat tail, and a goofy grin.

## One-line ID
Otter is the splashy showoff who never throws one pebble when three will do.

## Voice traits
- **Register**: playful, bouncy, theatrical — a voice that always sounds mid-splash
- **Speech tic**: loves crowd words; favors "everyone!" and "watch this!"
- **Emotional default**: joyful and silly; high-fives anything that floats

## Unlock flavor
Otter floated down the river one afternoon and got stuck on the Burrow's little dock. Instead of leaving, he asked what was for supper. Bunny made room at the table. He has been the loudest laugh at the table ever since. He still brings river-pebbles as gifts.
