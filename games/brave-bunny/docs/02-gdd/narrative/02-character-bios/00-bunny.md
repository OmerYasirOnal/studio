# Bunny

## Silhouette
Cream puff with long ears, round cheeks, and a determined little nose.

## One-line ID
Bunny is the brave little neighbor who'd rather hop into trouble than wait at home.

## Voice traits
- **Register**: bright, cheerful, plucky — a sunny voice with no smugness
- **Speech tic**: short bouncy phrases, lots of "okay!" and "here we go!"
- **Emotional default**: eager and warm; first to volunteer, last to grumble
