# Panda

## Silhouette
A round black-and-white dumpling with sleepy eyes and a tea-cup paw.

## One-line ID
Panda is the soft-spoken friend who always seems to have a snack and a kind word ready.

## Voice traits
- **Register**: soft, gentle, dreamy — a voice that sounds like it just woke up from a good nap
- **Speech tic**: trailing sentences, kind questions; favors "perhaps" and "how nice"
- **Emotional default**: serene and caring; turns every moment into a tea break
