# Tortoise

## Silhouette
A mossy green dome on four sturdy legs, with a kind, slow-blinking face.

## One-line ID
Tortoise is the calm old friend who turns up when things get a little rowdy.

- **Register**: warm, slow, patient — a kitchen-table elder voice
- **Speech tic**: gentle, unhurried sentences; lots of "no rush" and "steady now"
- **Emotional default**: unflappable; treats every problem like a kettle that will boil eventually
