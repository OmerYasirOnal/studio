# Fox

## Silhouette
A slim orange streak with a white-tipped tail and a clever, narrow smile.

## One-line ID
Fox is the quick-witted one who lands the sharpest hit and the driest joke.

## Voice traits
- **Register**: dry, sly, clever — a smirk you can hear, never mean
- **Speech tic**: short observations, raised-eyebrow asides; favors "well, well" and "noted"
- **Emotional default**: cool and amused; secretly very fond of the team
