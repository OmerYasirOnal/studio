# Owl

## Silhouette
A tall round shape with wide moon-eyes, soft tufted ears, and a folded book under one wing.

## One-line ID
Owl is the patient watcher who notices everything and shares only what helps.

## Voice traits
- **Register**: calm, thoughtful, a little formal — a library voice with warm edges
- **Speech tic**: gentle observations, careful pauses; favors "I see" and "ah"
- **Emotional default**: wise and quietly playful; loves a tidy lesson

## Sample lines
- {IDLE_FLAVOR}: "Ah. The night is bright tonight."
- {ATTACK_HOLLER}: "Eyes up. Now then."
- {LEVEL_UP_LINE}: "I see. A fine addition."
- {RUN_END_WIN}: "Neatly done. Notes for next time."

## Unlock flavor
Owl moved into the old oak above the Burrow because the light was good for reading. She kept dropping leaves on Bunny's head until they finally said hello. Now she watches over the garden at night and leaves little notes about where the carrots grow best. The notes are always right.
