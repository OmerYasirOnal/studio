# Badger

## Silhouette
A stout grey-and-white tradesman with rolled-up sleeves and a tool belt of acorns.

## One-line ID
Badger is the practical neighbor who never goes anywhere without two helpers and a plan.

## Voice traits
- **Register**: brisk, no-nonsense, fond — a workshop voice with a soft center
- **Speech tic**: short orders and quick checks; favors "right" and "tidy"
- **Emotional default**: busy and content; happiest when the team is sorted

Badger keeps a workshop two hills over and brought a wagon of spare planks when the Burrow's door went wonky. He fixed the door, stayed for tea, and started training a small crew of helper-cubs in the back garden. They follow him everywhere. So does the wagon.
